Milk Toast Taco Ideas:

  • Vehicle Damage: Every vehicle part has a value from 1-10. High damage can cause leaks or malfunctions!
  • Vehicle Buying/Selling/Reselling: Buy vehicles at dealerships or online. Prices are calculated based on installed parts and slightly randomized. Selling works similarly, with NPC offers depending on price.
  • Vehicle Parts System: Install aftermarket parts via mechanics or DIY garages. Multiplayer shops adjust prices dynamically based on demand. Watch out for scams and legality issues!
  • Police System: Police patrol areas, issue fines, pursue suspects, and adapt tactics. Laws vary by state, and false trials are possible!
  • Economy & Trade System: Prices depend on supply, demand, and NPC decisions. Small changes can ripple across towns and states!
  • NPC System: NPCs have traits and personalities affecting jobs, interactions, witnessing events, rumors, and union activity.
  • Construction & Terrain: Dig, build, and modify terrain anywhere. Cut and fill for foundations, move materials, and manage construction projects.
  • Property & Real Estate System: Buy property from agents or online. NPCs start with land to simulate a living economy.
  • Insurance System: Vehicle insurance covers crashes, theft, and more. Premiums vary by behavior, vehicle, and area. Fraud and scams exist!
  • Fuel & Energy System: Fuel consumption depends on terrain, vehicle, and driving style. Stations vary in price and availability. DIY fuel depots possible.

Ideas Im Working On:

  • Vehicle History Reports: Track owners, accidents, modifications. Some may be forged.
  • NPC Gossip System: Reputation spreads organically.
  • NPC Unions: Workers strike if conditions are bad.
  • Dynamic News System: Newspapers and radio report on events, markets, and player crimes.
  • Dynamic Pursuits: Police roadblocks, spike strips, helicopters, traffic tactics.
  • Vehicle History Forgery: Legal consequences if discovered.
  • Weather Impact: Roads, mud, and terrain affect vehicles, police, and NPC behavior.
